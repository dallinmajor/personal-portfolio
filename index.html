Toyota.com Clone

A clone of the "build and price" experience on Toyota's offical site.

I developed this site based off the purpose to get a customer to actually have an experience similar to going to a dealership and picking out a car to drive. The end goal is for a person to buy the car at a dealership by emailing their info and "build" to a dealer.

You can explore the site here.

  • Date: May 2017
  • Category: SEAN Stack (SQL, ExpressJS, AngularJS, NodeJS)

Charmi

A mobile-web based dating application modeled after apps like Tinder or Mutual.

Charmi is desgined with mobile use in mind. This partiucular styling was designed for an iPhone 6 Plus specifically in mind. Users can login with Auth0 (Gmail, Facebook etc.) and search profiles. Users can like or pass on profiles and then messages those they match with.

You can preview Charmi here.

  • Date: June/July 2017
  • Tech: React, Redux, Axios, jQuery, Socket.io, Node, SQL
